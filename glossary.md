Smart Money Concepts (SMC): A trading methodology focusing on the behavior of institutional market makers rather than retail indicators.
Liquidity Pools: Areas on a price chart where a large number of stop-loss orders are resting, often targeted by larger market participants.
Order Block (OB): The last bearish candle before a strong bullish move, or the last bullish candle before a strong bearish move.
Fair Value Gap (FVG): Imbalances in price action created by aggressive buying or selling, leaving a gap in the chart.
